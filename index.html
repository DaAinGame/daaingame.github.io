About

Chasing light
in unlikely places

Based in Kansas, I photograph the world from the ground up — and the sky down. Whether I'm tracking a nebula across a clear Midwestern night or waiting on a wildlife subject to move into frame, the goal is the same: a moment worth keeping.

This is a personal project and a living archive. New work goes up as it gets made.
